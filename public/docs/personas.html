User Personas

Overview

Your Projects at IBM serves three distinct user personas, each with unique needs, workflows, and capabilities. Understanding these personas helps you navigate the platform effectively.

Persona Comparison

  • Executives
  • Managers
  • Interns

Strategic Leaders

Executives are senior leaders who define organizational ambitions and monitor talent pipeline health. They focus on strategic initiatives and high-level metrics.

Primary Goals - Define strategic ambitions aligned with business objectives - Monitor talent pipeline and project portfolio health - Make data-driven decisions about resource allocation - Ensure projects align with organizational priorities

Key Responsibilities - Create and manage strategic ambitions - Review executive dashboard analytics - Configure AI services (BYOK) - Oversee project success metrics - Guide talent strategy

Access Level: Full platform access with administrative capabilities

Quick Stats - Dashboard: Executive - Create: Ambitions, Projects - AI Tools: Full Access - Analytics: Organization-wide

Team Leaders

Managers are team leaders who create project opportunities, review applications, and build teams. They focus on finding the right talent for specific initiatives.

Primary Goals - Create compelling project opportunities - Attract qualified candidates - Review and select the best talent - Build high-performing teams

Key Responsibilities - Post project opportunities with clear requirements - Review intern applications - Use AI to match resumes to roles - Accept/decline applications with feedback - Track project staffing progress

Access Level: Project management and team-level analytics

Quick Stats - Dashboard: Manager - Create: Projects - AI Tools: Resume Matching - Analytics: Project-level

Detailed Manager Guide ‚Üí

Talented Contributors

Interns are skilled individuals seeking meaningful project work aligned with their career goals. They focus on discovering opportunities and showcasing their abilities.

Primary Goals - Discover projects aligned with skills and interests - Apply to compelling opportunities - Track application status - Build experience and skills

Key Responsibilities - Maintain up-to-date profile and resume - Browse and apply to projects - Respond to application updates - Communicate with project leaders - Complete assigned projects

Access Level: Personal dashboard and project discovery

Quick Stats - Dashboard: Personal - Apply: To Projects - AI Tools: Recommendations - Analytics: Personal stats

Role-Based Navigation

How to Identify Your Role

When you log in, your role is displayed in the header next to your email:

  • Purple Badge: Leader/Manager
  • Cyan Badge: Intern
  • Gray Badge: Other roles

Your role determines which features and dashboards you can access.

Common Questions

Q: Can I have multiple roles?
A: Yes, some users may have both Executive and Manager capabilities. Your highest privilege level determines your access.

Q: How do I switch between dashboards?
A: Use the navigation menu to access different dashboard views based on your role.

Q: Can interns see executive dashboards?
A: No, dashboards are role-restricted to ensure appropriate data access.

Q: How are roles assigned?
A: Roles are assigned based on your IBM organizational position and are managed by system administrators.
